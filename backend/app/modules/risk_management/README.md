# Risk Management Module

## Purpose

Enforces risk controls, monitors exposure, and helps traders stay within
their predefined risk parameters.

## Responsibilities

- Position sizing calculations (fixed fractional, Kelly, etc.)
- Daily/weekly loss limits and drawdown alerts
- Correlation and concentration monitoring
- Risk-report generation

## Allowed Dependencies

- `app.core` (config, database, dependencies)
